Crafting a Standout Resume: Step-by-Step Guide

Learn how to build a professional resume that stands out to employers!

Materials Needed

  • Computer with text editing software (e.g., Microsoft Word, Google Docs, Canva)
  • List of work experiences, skills, and accomplishments
  • Access to resume templates or design tools
  • PDF reader for saving and reviewing your resume
  • Grammarly or other proofreading tool
  • Sample job descriptions for targeting your resume
  • A quiet workspace to focus on resume-building

Instructions

Step 1: Choose a Resume Format

Decide on a format based on your experience level and job field:

  • Chronological Format: Lists experience in reverse order. Best for solid work history.
  • Functional Format: Highlights skills over jobs. Great for limited experience or career changers.
  • Combination Format: A mix of both; ideal for those with relevant skills and experience.

Step 2: Add Contact Information

Include essential details at the top of the resume:

  • Full Name
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • LinkedIn or relevant social links (optional)

Step 3: Write a Strong Summary or Objective

Summarize your professional background or career goals, tailored to the job:

  • Summary: An overview for those with experience, highlighting key skills and achievements.
  • Objective: Ideal for newcomers, focusing on career goals and value to the employer.

Step 4: List Work Experience

Detail your work history, starting with the most recent role:

  • Job Title, Company Name, and Location
  • Dates of Employment
  • Responsibilities and Achievements (quantify where possible)

Step 5: Highlight Education

Mention your educational background, including relevant coursework or certifications:

  • Degree, Major, School Name
  • Graduation Date
  • GPA or honors (optional)

Step 6: Showcase Skills

List skills relevant to the job:

  • Hard Skills: Technical skills, software, or languages.
  • Soft Skills: Interpersonal skills like communication and teamwork.

Step 7: Add Additional Sections (Optional)

Consider adding these sections to stand out:

  • Certifications and Training
  • Volunteer Experience
  • Projects

Step 8: Proofread and Format

Review for consistency, errors, and formatting. Save as a PDF to preserve layout.

Finalizing Your Resume

Your resume is now polished and ready to impress potential employers! Good luck with your job search!
